Current CAT Exam Structure

Section Questions Time Limit Max Marks Question Types
Verbal Ability & Reading Comp. (VARC) 24 40 min 72 16 RC + 8 VA (Para Jumbles, Summaries)
Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning (DILR) 20 40 min 60 4 Sets of 5 Qs each
Quantitative Ability (QA) 22 40 min 66 Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Numbers
Total 66 120 min 198 MCQs + Non-MCQs (TITA)

Marking Scheme: +3 for correct, -1 for incorrect MCQ, 0 for TITA (Type in the Answer) and unattempted.
Strict Sectional Timings: No section switching allowed.

The 50% Rule

You do not need to solve the whole paper. Scoring exactly half the marks (approx. 99 out of 198) usually guarantees a 99.5+ percentile. Focus heavily on accuracy.

Strategies for 99+ Percentile

Accuracy > Number of attempts

1. The 'ABC' Strategy

  • Attempt: Easy questions first.
  • Bookmark: Medium questions to return to.
  • Commit to Leave: Hard/lengthy questions skip immediately.

2. Section Tactics

VARC: Read diverse articles (Aeon/NYT) daily.
DILR: Set selection is everything. Pick 2 easy sets out of 4.
QA: Arithmetic and Algebra make up 65% of the section.

3. Mock Analysis Loop

  • Spend 3 hours analyzing a 2-hour mock.
  • Maintain an "Error Log" notebook.
  • Re-solve unattempted questions without a timer.
