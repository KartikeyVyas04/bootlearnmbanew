Tips to Score 99+ Percentile

Accuracy > Number of attempts

1. Master Time Management

  • Scan quickly for easiest questions first.
  • Skip lengthy calculations—come back later.
  • Take regular timed sectionals.

2. Section Strategies

VARC: Focus on RCs; read Qs before passages.
DILR: Identify easier sets, attempt TITA first.
QA: Master Arithmetic, practice mental math.

3. Practice Smart Mocks

  • Analyze all solutions after mocks.
  • Log errors and create revision sheets.
  • Simulate exam environment slots.

4. Maximize Accuracy

  • Aim for >75% accuracy; avoid wild guesses.
  • TITA has no negative marking, solve more.

5. Build Fundamentals

  • Master basics before advanced topics.
  • Read diverse opinions for VARC growth.

6. Attempt Selection

  • Choose confident questions.
  • Don't attempt all, maximize marks selectively.
